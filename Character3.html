ACTOR

Shameik Moore

Shameik Alti Moore

      He is an American actor and musician. He made his lead acting debut in Dope, and is best known for voicing Miles Morales / Spider-Man in the animated film Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse and its sequels. Born in Atlanta of Jamaican descent, Shameik Moore booked his first lead role of Malcolm in Rick Famuyiwa's Dope (2015), produced by Forest Whitaker and Nina Yang Bongiovi. The film, which is narrated by Forest Whitaker, also stars Tony Revolori, Kiersey Clemons, Zoë Kravitz, Kimberly Elise and A$AP Rocky. - Wikipedia



Born: May 4, 1995 (age 28 years), Atlanta, Georgia, United States
Height: 1.7 m
Parents: Errol Moore, Sharon Moore
Other names: King SAM
Films: Spider-Man: Homecoming, Spide-Man: Far From Home, Spider-Man: No Way Home
