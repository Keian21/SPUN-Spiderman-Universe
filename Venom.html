Venom (2018)

Release date:  October 4, 2018 (Philippines)
Director: Ruben Fleischer
Genre: Action , Adventure , Fantasy , Sci-Fi
Distributed by: Sony Pictures Releasing
Based on: Marvel Comics

   Summary:

One of the countless symbiotes that have invaded Earth, an alien creature, bonds with a failed reporter. However, the creature grows fond of Earth and chooses to defend it.Eddie Brock is a former reporter whose life is in shambles after a botched interview with the Life Foundation destroys his career. When he returns to the Life Foundation after a six-month absence, he gets into contact with an extraterrestrial symbiote and transforms into the parasitic antihero Venom.

Famous journalist and reporter Eddie Brock (Tom Hardy) "follows people who don't want to be followed." He learns about the Life Foundation and loses his entire career trying to find out what accusations the foundation was subjected to. Eddie is taken back to the foundation six months later by one of their top scientists, but during his return, he encounters an extraterrestrial creature called a symbiote. The Symbiote named Venom decides to cling to Eddie's body and adapt, but this brings Brock into conflict with the Life Foundation's chief, Carlton Drake (Riz Ahmed). Eddie/Venom needs to stop Drake from using his invasion forces to transform the humans into complete symbiotes in large numbers.

With his back to the wall, erstwhile idealist reporter Eddie Brock is defeated and without anybody to turn to after deciding to pursue the evil Life Foundation Corporation and its visionary CEO, Carlton Drake. Six difficult months later, Eddie, who is now homeless, discovers Drake's horrifying secret about his unethical experiments on humans. However, in the process, he unintentionally becomes the host of a weird, amorphous, pitch-black alien life form known as Venom, a potent interplanetary symbiote. Eddie now has to maintain the life of the ravenous parasite, whether he likes it or not, for the megalomaniacal Drake's deadly scheme is set to take Earth down. Can Eddie, though, withstand the venom inside?
